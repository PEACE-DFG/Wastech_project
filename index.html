About Electricmate

Electricmate is a sophisticated web application designed to meet the intricate demands of determining the ideal wire size tailored to precise load requirements. It serves as an indispensable tool for electrical professionals and enthusiasts alike.

Our application provides comprehensive solutions for sizing wires with unparalleled accuracy, ensuring optimal performance and paramount safety in all electrical installations.

Industrial Wire Sizing

Electricmate's industrial wire sizing solutions are engineered for demanding industrial environments, manufacturing facilities, and heavy machinery applications. Our application provides precise calculations to ensure your electrical systems can handle industrial loads while maintaining safety and efficiency.

Industrial applications require special consideration for factors like motor starting currents, voltage drop over long distances, ambient temperature variations, and compliance with industrial electrical standards. Electricmate handles all these complexities to deliver accurate wire sizing recommendations.

  • Compliant with industrial electrical standards
  • Calculations for motor loads and heavy equipment
  • Consideration of voltage drop and temperature factors
  • Solutions for all industrial applications

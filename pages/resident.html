Residential Wire Sizing

Electricmate's residential wire sizing solutions are designed for homes, apartments, and living spaces. Our application provides precise calculations to ensure your electrical systems meet household needs while maintaining the highest safety standards.

Residential applications require consideration of factors like typical household appliance loads, lighting circuits, kitchen and laundry requirements, and compliance with residential electrical codes. Electricmate handles all these factors to deliver accurate wire sizing recommendations for your home.
